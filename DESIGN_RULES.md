- Text: gray-900 / gray-600 / gray-400

- H3 (card title): text-base font-semibold

- Caption: text-xs text-gray-400

### Components
- All cards: rounded-2xl
- All buttons: rounded-xl
- Page padding: p-8
- Card padding: p-6
